# Cortex — Live Demo Path

_The shortest click-path that makes the biggest impression. ~4 minutes._

1. **Open `/grow` (Overview).** Point at the **health chip** and **Triage Strip**:
   "The system already looked at my goals and pipeline and told me the three
   highest-value things to do right now — ranked, with the reason each one
   matters." Tap **Do this now** on the top item → the AI Coach answers instantly.

2. **Tap a KPI chip → set goals.** Show that income / leads / close-rate goals
   drive the whole thing, and they're the *same* goals as the Commission Planner —
   one number, everywhere.

3. **Go to Lead Gen.** "These are my real partner leads, scored and routed." Point
   at the **proactive AI insight** ("your coach already checked your pipeline") and
   tap **Follow-up** on a hot lead → the coach drafts outreach on the spot.

4. **Go to AI Team.** This is the showstopper. "Every automation is a real agent
   with a real, copyable system prompt and a build guide." Expand **Lead Scoring
   Agent** → show the full prompt + **Copy**. Then scroll up and hit **Copy Setup
   Prompt** — "paste that into Claude and it walks me through building all 18."

5. **Go to Automations.** Flip one agent's status (as a manager) and open its card:
   the **ROI stat + source**, the **tool-connection status**, and the **tunable
   config**. "This is the operational view of the same team."

6. **Go to Content Engine.** Show the **Next Move** card, tap **why this number**
   to reveal the EV math, then **Download Creator Playbook** — a real file. "It
   tells me exactly what to post to attract agencies, and why it's worth my time."

7. **Close on the Feedback button** (top of any Growth page): "And it improves
   itself — I rate what's working, it rolls into a manager digest, approved
   changes update the agents. No developer in the loop."

**One-liner to land it:** "It's not a dashboard that shows me numbers — it's a
team of assistants that does the work, tells me the next move, and gets smarter
every week."
